EHT FBLA

Since it was reactivated in 2005, EHT FBLA has become more successful every year. In the 2019-2020 school year, we reached a record breaking amount of members and on the summer of 2019 we made history with our first first place Nationals win.

NLC

Tens of thousands of FBLA Members all over the world will gather in Seattle, Washington for the 2024 National Leadership Conference from June 29th to July 3rd. During this week, we will be able to travel the city, participate in national, regional, and state meetings, compete, and network with other FBLA members.
